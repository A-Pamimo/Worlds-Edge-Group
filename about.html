About Us

Our story and the people behind World’s Edge Group

Our Story

World’s Edge Group was founded in Western Canada with a singular goal: to deliver evidence‑based insights that empower organisations to navigate the dynamic landscape of economics, policy and strategy. The name reflects our commitment to operating at the frontier—where new ideas emerge, horizons expand and data drives better decisions. We work with business leaders, governments, investors and non‑profits to illuminate hidden opportunities, anticipate change and chart sustainable paths forward.

Our multidisciplinary practice brings together economists, data scientists and strategists who share a passion for impactful research and pragmatic solutions. Whether shaping public policy, designing community dashboards or advising on strategic pivots, we combine rigorous analysis with a deep understanding of the region’s unique context.

Founder’s Profile

Pamimo Akinjide is the founder and principal of World’s Edge Group.
