Impact 100 Report

The Impact 100 Report is our flagship research programme for the Western Canadian Economic Forum. It profiles the top 100 companies shaping the region’s economy and analyses how they drive growth, innovation and community well‑being. Our methodology combines quantitative indicators such as revenue, employment and investment with qualitative assessments of leadership and sustainability to provide a holistic view of regional impact.

The first edition of the report will be released soon. Sign up to receive updates and gain early access to insights that will help investors, policy‑makers and business leaders stay ahead of the curve.

City of Saskatoon: Drinking Water Advisory Dashboard

We partnered with the City of Saskatoon to develop a public dashboard that delivers timely information on drinking water advisories. The dashboard links to the City’s internal systems and displays current advisories (yellow), lifted advisories (green) and emergency boil‑water orders (red). Residents can quickly see addresses impacted, the cause and the date issued 【119511891186112†L148-L164】. The tool is updated twice daily, providing transparency and improving community resilience.
